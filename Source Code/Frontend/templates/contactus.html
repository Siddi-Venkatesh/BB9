Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access your datasets?

All our datasets are freely available for download from our website. Simply navigate to the Datasets section and choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, or Excel).

Can I use your data for commercial projects?

Yes, our datasets are released under open licenses that allow both academic and commercial use. Please review the specific license for each dataset.

Do you offer research collaborations?

Absolutely! We're always interested in collaborating with researchers and institutions. Contact us with your proposal and we'll be in touch.

How often are datasets updated?

We regularly update our datasets to ensure accuracy and relevance. Major updates are typically released quarterly, with minor updates as needed.

Can I contribute data or research?

Yes! We welcome contributions from the community. Please reach out through our contact form to discuss how you can contribute.

Do you provide technical support?

We offer community support through our documentation and forums. For specific technical issues, please email our support team.
